About This Project

The Medical Cost Prediction application is designed to estimate medical insurance costs based on various personal factors. This project demonstrates the application of machine learning in the healthcare insurance domain.

The prediction model analyzes several key factors that influence insurance costs, including age, BMI, number of children, smoking status, and geographic region. By understanding these factors, users can gain insights into what affects their insurance premiums.

Random Forest Regressor

Our prediction model uses a Random Forest algorithm, which combines multiple decision trees to make accurate predictions.

86% Accuracy

The model achieves an RÂ² score of 0.86, indicating strong predictive performance on insurance cost data.

Real-World Data

Trained on a dataset of real medical insurance costs to provide realistic estimates.

Features

  • BMI Calculator: Calculate your Body Mass Index directly in the application
  • Cost Prediction: Get an estimate of your medical insurance costs
